ABOUT

I'm Eric, a React/Redux developer with my sights set on building the future. I'm all about creating intuitive and interesting apps around creative, data-driven ideas, especially those with educational or research applications. I'm interested in anything that has the potential to push the envelope and change how we think.

I'm a Brooklyn native with a bachelor's degree in linguistics as well as past lives in the book publishing industry and as a karaoke bartender. My bookshelf is full of fantasy and science fiction, and I'm always looking to learn more about psycholinguistics, natural language processing, machine learning, and sociopolitics in Asian American and LGBTQIA+ spaces.
