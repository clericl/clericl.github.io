Catena is an etymology mapper. Given a word, Catena finds all of its ancestors and then finds all the words derived from each ancestor. Catena then draws out the connections between these words. The largest, innermost nodes are the ancestors furthest back, and each linked node is a word derived from that ancestor, branching out in turn to its own descendants.
